Case thickness affects comfort and style. Thinner cases are more elegant, while thicker cases offer more presence.

44mm+ diameter

How to Measure Your Wrist

  1. Use a flexible measuring tape or string
  2. Wrap around your wrist just below the wrist bone
  3. Make sure it's snug but not tight
  4. Note the measurement in inches or centimeters
Pro Tips:
  • Measure at the end of the day when wrists are largest
  • Consider your style preference (loose vs snug fit)
  • Account for seasonal swelling
